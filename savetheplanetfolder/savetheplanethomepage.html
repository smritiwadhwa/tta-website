In the words of our group, saving the planet is important for several reasons. Our generation is the future of not only this country, but also the globe, and the way we take care of it is incredibly important. Educating not only ourselves, but making such information available to other children of our age is vital to instill a sense of care about the environment.

To not only educate ourselves about current harmful actions towards our Earth, but also take some action against it, visit the "How to Help" tabs and we encourage our peers to research as well. While we are young, we may not be able to make a physical difference, but learning about the subject can increase interest to make change in the future.
