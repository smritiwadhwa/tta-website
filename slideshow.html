COVID-19 and Tech Team Arcadia
As you all may know, there is an increasing concern of the lack of essential supplies to last this Covid-19 outbreak. Paper towels, toilet paper, hand sanitizer, and disinfectant wipes, to say the least, have been swiped away in panic during the early hours of the day, causing more individuals to have to go “hunting” for these items. The increased travel elevates human contact and defeats the purpose of social distancing. To avoid people from staying out for too long, we have created a supplies tracker for the Arcadia area listing what is available where.
